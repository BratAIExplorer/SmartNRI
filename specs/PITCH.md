# SmartNRI — Founder Pitch

**One line:** The truth before the transaction.

---

## The Problem

18 million Indians live abroad. Every single one of them faces the same nightmare: navigating TWO governments at once — India's rules AND their host country's rules. Tax, visas, banking, property, compliance.

Today, they get their information from:
- **WhatsApp groups** — unverified rumours from strangers
- **Fintech apps** — banks in disguise that want to sell products
- **Government portals** — 50-page PDFs written in legalese
- **Expensive lawyers** — $200/hour for answers that should be free

There is no single, trusted, free place where an NRI can find verified government rules — across countries — without someone trying to sell them something.

---

## The Solution

**SmartNRI is the Google Maps of NRI compliance.** We don't build the roads. We show you exactly where they are.

An AI pipeline scrapes government websites across 7 countries every day, summarizes regulatory updates into 3-bullet cards, and publishes them on a clean mobile-first site — with a direct link to the official source on every single card.

```
Government publishes update → AI reads it → AI summarizes it →
Card appears on SmartNRI with source link → NRI reads it in 30 seconds
```

No human editor needed. No opinions. No product sales. Just verified facts with source links.
